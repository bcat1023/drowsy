Intro

Hey there, I'm drowsy. I was born in 2008; my dad liked computers and he would do all sorts of things. I watched him and wanted to do what he did, and from there my love for computers grew. Not too long later I started coding and messing around with computers. My first line of code written was actually on a old computer I got from EcoThrift, It ran windowsXP and had a website stored on it called VAIO Support. It was intended for people to get access to support, however instead of using it I modified it and changed the text to other things. From there I googled html tutorial and found a website called w3schools, I followed and read the whole tutorial and made my first website. Years later here I am working on big projects like AACDN, and Titan Network.
